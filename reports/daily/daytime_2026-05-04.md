## Interpretation

The lower synthetic weight stabilized the two-seed pilot behavior, but the full
seed set does not support promotion. Seeds 43, 44, and 47 improved; seeds 42,
45, 46, 48, and 49 regressed. The seed 47 gain is not enough to overcome the
larger losses on seeds 42 and 45.

This confirms the current data augmentation path does not solve the ACCORD
ceiling. The bottleneck remains encoder capacity or true data volume, not
synthetic-sample mixing under the tested setup.
